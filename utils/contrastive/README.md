## **CLEAN-Derived Files**
The following files in the `contrastive` directory are derived from the **CLEAN** software,  
developed by the **Department of Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign**:

- `dataloader.py`
- `distance_map.py`
- `evaluate.py`
- `infer.py`
